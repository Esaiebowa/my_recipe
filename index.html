Crispy Chicken Poutine

Introduction

Crispy Chicken Poutine is a delicious variation of the classic Quebecois poutine recipe. It perfectly combines the crispiness of chicken with the smoothness of cheese curds and savory brown gravy.

Ingredients
  • Yukon Gold potatoes, cut into fries
  • Chicken breasts, cut into pieces
  • Fresh cheese curds
  • Brown gravy
  • Flour
  • Beaten egg
  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Frying oil
Preparation
  1. Preheat your frying oil to 180Â°C.
  2. Season chicken pieces with salt and pepper. Coat them in flour, then dip them in beaten egg and finally in panko breadcrumbs.
  3. Fry the chicken pieces until golden and crispy. Drain them on paper towels.
  4. Fry the potato fries until golden and crispy. Also drain them on paper towels.
  5. In individual bowls, distribute cooked potato fries, crispy chicken pieces, and cheese curds. Pour hot brown gravy over them.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy this delicious Crispy Chicken Poutine!
